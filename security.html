Passwords

This is the first line of defense in security.
Simple passwords have lowercase letters only and are quick to crack.
Complex passwords have Upper & lowercase letters, numbers & special
characters and are EXPONENTIALLY  More difficult to crack.

takes 6 months to crack as opposed to 4 hours for all lowercase letters.

takes 15,368,300 years to crack as opposed to 210 years for all lowercase letters.
